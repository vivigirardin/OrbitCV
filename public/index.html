Career Profile

I am a new graduate with a Master’s degree in toxicology from the University of Oslo. My master’s thesis was about the origin and bioaccumulation of organic pollutants from traffic in dragonfly larvae. My Master’s thesis was funded by the Norwegian Public Roads Administration and the Norwegian Institute for Water Research (NIVA). Last summer, I worked in the section for climate and environment at Statens vegvesen (the Norwegian Public Roads Administration). Previously, I worked as a research assistant at NIVA. The collaboration with NIVA continues, and I was hired to summarize my thesis for publication in a scientific journal. As a person, I am solution-oriented, committed and creative. I am also a hardworking and goal-oriented person who, among other things, continues with the studies without postponement despite the fact that I have become the mother of my second child in the middle of my studies.
